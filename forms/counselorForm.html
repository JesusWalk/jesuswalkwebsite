Counselor Application Form

1 General Information

Responsibilities

  1. Providing spiritual and personal guidance to students while being a role model and guardian for the duration of conference and after.
  2. Be openly communicative to co-counselors and JesusWalk staff members.
  3. Facilitate a caring, safe and fun space for students.
  4. Expected to stay on campus for the whole duration of the conference.
  5. Must follow all given rules by the JesusWalk Youth Conference Organization as well as Hope International University.

Requirements and Qualifications

  1. Must be confident in your identity in Christ.
  2. Must be a baptized believer and an active member in your church.
  3. Must have high integrity and flexibility in a hectic environment.
  4. Must be a team player and a self-starter.
  5. Must have interpersonal and communication skills.
  6. Must be present throughout the duration of the conference.

About JesusWalk

  1. Mission Statement
    The JesusWalk Youth Conference is designed to open the eyes of our youth to the potential they have in Christ. Our vision is that every student breaks from their worldly chains to become who God has designed them to be—each in their own way a disciple furthering His Kingdom. Our mission at the JesusWalk Youth Conference is to provide an open and safe refuge for youth to experience God and His Word. We accomplish this through a four-day dynamic program that includes moving worship, engaging messages, community service, and extensive mentoring by small group interaction. 1 John 2:6 says, “Whoever claims to live in him must live as Jesus did.” We give our students a clear picture of how to move beyond the conference—influenced and driven by the Gospel to continually live in His example. What drives JesusWalk is the people who help make it happen. We’ve built this conference with our own youth & young adults to work together as one body and strive for one goal: transform the lives of our students. With the guidance of the CCMP, JesusWalk continues to grow and find new ways of reaching out.
  2. JesusWalk is a 4-day youth conference designed to open the eyes of our youth to the potential they have in Christ by breaking free from their worldly chains to become who God has designed them to be in furthering His Kingdom.
