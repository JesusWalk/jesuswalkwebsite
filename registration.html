We're really excited to have you register for JesusWalk. Before you begin, please be sure to read through the Registration Policy and have your payment information ready. Please click below to begin the registration process.

FOR STUDENTS

WHEN YOU LEAVE THIS CONFERENCE, WE BELIEVE YOU WILL HAVE A DEEPER RELATIONSHIP WITH GOD AND A CLEAR UNDERSTANDING OF HOW TO BE A LIGHT WITHIN YOUR CHURCH AND COMMUNITY.

THINGS YOU'LL NEED TO KNOW:

    FOOD & LODGING

  • Food and lodging IS INCLUDED in the registration. You'll be provided meals for the entire conference. If you have any food allergies, please be sure to list it when you register and/or let our registration team know of your allergies.
  • It is recommended you bring snacks because you will not be allowed to leave the campus at any time during the conference unless it is an emergency.
  • You will need to bring pillows and bed sheets/sleeping bag. These will NOT be provided in the dorm rooms.

    MONEY

  • You may want to bring money for conference t-shirts, misc items and emergency purposes.

    IN CASE OF EMERGENCY

  • A nurse will be onsite throughout the conference hours. Our nurse is equipped to handle all minor incidents.
  • In case of emergency, if you need to reach your child during the conference, please call 657-234-5992.
  • You should inform our registration team of your child's allergies, medications, or other medical conditions. We would suggest that your youth pastor and chaperones are made aware as well.

FOR PARENTS

JesusWalk Youth Conference is an opportunity for junior high, high school and college students to get away for four days and grow like Jesus did in Luke 2:52 - "And Jesus grew in wisdom and stature, and in favor with God and men."

Our conference is designed to open the eyes of our youth to the message of the Gospel on how to live their life in a material world. We want to show the youth how to live in this world, but not to be of it. With a dynamic program consisting of powerful worship, engaging messages, community service projects and extensive mentoring through small group interaction, young people will leave the conference with a deeper relationship with God and a clear understanding of how to be a light within their church and community.You will need to bring pillows and bed sheets/sleeping bag. These will NOT be provided in the dorm rooms.

JW COST

In the current economy, we know money is tight. That’s why we do everything we can to make the JesusWalk experience the most affordable for all our students.

We understand it's hard to take your youth group to a youth conference. Especially if you're a small church and having difficulties finding a budget for attending. We've always said don't let money be the reason why you can't attend JesusWalk. Which is why we've worked hard to keep costs down, but also to provide your youth group with the most impactful program to serve you, and further God's Kingdom!

2016 STUDENT COST

The student cost for JesusWalk covers admission for 4 days; three nights lodging, breakfast, lunch and dinner for all 4 days. Also included is the welcome back with all the materials needed for the conference.

  • $185* = Individual cost

  • $180* = For groups of 5 or more
