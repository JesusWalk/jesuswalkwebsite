Our theme this year is "This Is It”. The culture we live in today teaches us that we have to earn everything. We constantly have to prove our worth and value as individuals and must strive to be someone and not just a face in the crowd. This summer, we will be diving into what it means to be the Church. As John 3:16 says, “For God so loved the WORLD, that He gave His only Son…” We quickly forget that Jesus did not just come to save the righteous but everyone in this world no matter who you are. And as followers of Jesus Christ, we are not alone but rather have been given the opportunity to be a part of a world changing force called the Church. This year at JesusWalk, we will be dedicating each day to going in depth on what this means:

This is it: We are Welcomed

Gospel presentation night: Jesus died so that I can become we and so that we can be forgiven no matter what. We are all invited and desired no matter what.

This is it: We are Changed

We may seem weird but what we do together and what we have experienced is crazy awesome and makes us better. Our devotion is counter-cultural but the miracles we experience remind us that He is with us. Some of this requires us to look back at what God has done so that he can push us forward together.

This is it: We are One

We do everything together. There is no "I" but rather we choose to live for the other. But what does that sacrifice mean? Can you give all of you? Do you mean it?

This is it: We are Committed

Sometimes it may feel repetitive but being the church is a lifestyle that changes the world one day at a time. We must persevere through it even when it’s tough. Are you in?
